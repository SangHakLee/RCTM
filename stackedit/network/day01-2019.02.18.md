**powershell**
```powershell

PS C:\Windows\system32> Get-Command -CommandType application | Where-Object {$_.Name -like "*.msc"}

PS C:\Windows\system32> Get-Command -CommandType application | Where-Object {$_.Name -like "*.exe"}
```
